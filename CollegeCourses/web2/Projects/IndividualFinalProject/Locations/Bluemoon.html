Bluemoon Tower

A massive tower in the north-east part of the kingdom. The tower is large enough that it can be seen from nearly anywhere in the kingdom. It's reachable only after braving the wild on a long jounery from the capital city, past the direwolves, orges, bandits, awakened golems, harpyies and hordes upon hordes of animiated skeletons! The view on the top is worth the brutal jounery.
