Oz is a very unique place in Kansas, USA that only appears in the eyes of tornados blowing through the state.

Oz is a one-of-a-kind horror-themed impossible surivial challege, uniquely tailored to each of its visitors for maximum horror!

The prize for suriving the unending nightmare is one (1) wish! There's no rules for this single wish, you can wish for anything you want, no matter how twisted and sick it is, and the wish will be granted no matter the cost!

However, as with all great reward, comes great risk! Will your sanity still be intact in the impossible event you survive the endless horrors of Oz?
