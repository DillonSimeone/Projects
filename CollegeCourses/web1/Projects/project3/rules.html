Rules

Upon arrival, all of your belongings and clothings will be taken, and you'll be given a jumpsuit to wear. Your clothings and items will be returned to you if you survive the nightmare. There's rules that you will have to aside by long as you're a visitor to the land of Oz.

  • No profanity or obscene gestures.
  • No illuminating devices permitted.
  • No speaking, you're allowed to scream, however.
  • You must stay on the red path at all time.
  • Have fun!

The consequence for breaking any of those rules is to suffer a fate worse than death.
