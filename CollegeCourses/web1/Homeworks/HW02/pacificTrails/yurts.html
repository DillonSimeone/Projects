The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our Luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luxury camping experience also includes elecricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothings.
